# Blogify Design System

## Theme

A restrained publishing studio. Product surfaces use neutral canvas and white writing surfaces; vermilion is reserved for actions, focus, and current selection. Marketing may use committed vermilion surfaces.

## Color

- Ink: `#17191F`
- Canvas: `#F6F7F5`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Subtle: `#ECEEEA`
- Line: `#D8DCD6`
- Muted: `#626970`
- Brand: `#B83A18`
- Brand bright: `#F06432`
- Success: `#16734A`
- Warning: `#8A5800`
- Danger: `#B42318`
- Info: `#245EA8`

## Typography

Use Source Sans 3 when available, with a system sans fallback. Product page titles are 32/38 and bold; section titles are 20/26; body is 16/24. Keep writing columns between 680px and 720px and prose below 70ch.

## Layout

Product content is capped at 1440px with 24px mobile and 32px desktop gutters. The sidebar is 248px. Use separators and grouped sections instead of nested cards. Shadows are reserved for floating surfaces.

## Components

Buttons and form controls have a minimum 44px target. Primary actions are solid vermilion; secondary actions use ink or borders. Statuses use quiet tinted backgrounds. Data lists are divided rows. Empty states state the condition, consequence, and next action.

## Motion

Controls transition in 160ms and floating surfaces in 220ms using ease-out. Reduced-motion mode removes transforms and nonessential transitions.
