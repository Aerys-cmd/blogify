# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Independent creators who want to write, publish, and understand an audience without managing technical infrastructure.

## Product Purpose

Blogify gives creators a focused publishing workspace and a polished home for their ideas. Success means a creator can move from account creation to a published first post with confidence and return regularly to write and understand readership.

## Brand Personality

Confident, articulate, crafted, calm under pressure, and creator-first.

## Anti-references

Blogify must not resemble a generic Bootstrap admin template, an interchangeable SaaS gradient landing page, or a metric-card dashboard. Avoid decorative gradients, emoji feature grids, generic dark sidebars, nested cards, and technical language in creator-facing copy.

## Design Principles

- Put writing first and make the next useful action obvious.
- Show creators the outcome, not the implementation.
- Use restraint to create confidence and preserve focus.
- Explain empty and error states with a clear recovery action.
- Keep workflows familiar, accessible, and predictable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard-only workflows, visible focus, reduced motion, touch targets of at least 44px, high-contrast text, and resilient English and Turkish layouts.
